Falmouth Food Co-Op

Overview

A prototype mobile app for Falmouth Food Co-Op, a community-run online grocery store featuring local, non-industrial farmers. Designed to simplify the current ordering process. Users have expressed the existing site being confusing and difficult to place orders on, making them reluctant to purchase from.



Colour Palette

Simplified their existing colour palette. Bright colourful, reflecting the range of produce they sell and the range of people that they work with.


Empathy Map

Conducted an interview with someone who had never used FFC but would be interested. Gained some valuable insight into new customer bases thoughts & feelings.

User Flow

Evaluated the current FFC ordering process based on their website. Simplified the order process to be more user-friendly and more suitable for an app.

Dark Skies App

Overview

We believe everyone should get to experience stargazing.

Stargazing is often inaccessible because good dark sky locations are hard to find - they're usually only shared by word of mouth or far-off observatories. Dark Skies is an app designed to make stargazing accessible for everyone by helping people discover safe, local dark sky locations.
